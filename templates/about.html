About YOUR CROP MATTERS

Empowering farmers with AI-powered crop disease detection

Our Mission

YOUR CROP MATTERS is dedicated to helping farmers and agricultural professionals identify crop diseases quickly and accurately. By leveraging artificial intelligence and machine learning, we provide a tool that can detect diseases in apple, corn, and tomato plants, helping to reduce crop losses and improve yields.

How It Works

Our application uses a trained deep learning model to analyze images of plant leaves. Simply upload a photo of your plant, and our system will identify potential diseases, provide a confidence score, and offer treatment and prevention recommendations.

Supported Crops

Currently, we can detect diseases in:

  • Apple trees (Apple scab, Black rot, Cedar apple rust)
  • Corn/Maize (Cercospora leaf spot, Common rust, Northern Leaf Blight)
  • Tomato plants (Early blight, Late blight, Leaf mold, and more)

Our Technology

Machine Learning

Powered by advanced neural networks trained on thousands of plant disease images.

Accessibility

Access our tool from any device with a browser and internet connection.

Knowledge Base

Comprehensive database of disease information and treatment recommendations.
