Frequently Asked Questions

What crops can your system detect diseases for?

Currently, our system can detect diseases in apple trees, corn/maize, and tomato plants. We're continuously working to expand our capabilities to more crops.

How accurate is the disease detection?

Our model has been trained on thousands of images and achieves high accuracy rates. However, we always recommend consulting with an agricultural expert for confirmation.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. Your uploaded images are used only for disease detection and are not shared with third parties.

Do you offer API access for integration?

Yes, we offer API access for businesses and developers who want to integrate our disease detection capabilities into their own applications. Contact us for details.
